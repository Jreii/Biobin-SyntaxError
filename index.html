Set Up & Connect Your Smart Bin

Place your smart biodegradable bin in your chosen location and follow the simple setup steps. Connect it to your account through the app or web platform to enable remote access and control.

Detect & Monitor NPK Levels

The bin automatically analyzes the organic waste to detect Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), and Potassium (K) levels. Data is collected in real time and stored securely in your account for easy access.

Track Insights Anytime, Anywhere

Log in to your dashboard from any device to monitor NPK data, track trends, and manage usage manually or remotely. Stay in control whether you're on-site or away.
